About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Missouri and a member of the Digital Biology Lab. My research sits at the intersection of large language models, biomedical text and figure mining, and knowledge graph construction, with the goal of building AI systems that help clinicians and scientists make better decisions.

Research Interests

A high-level view of topics I am excited about:

  • Prompt engineering and optimization for large language models.
  • Retrieval-augmented generation for biomedical literature and pathway figures.
  • Mining gene–disease and gene–drug relationships and building knowledge graphs.
  • AI methods that support clinical and translational research workflows.
