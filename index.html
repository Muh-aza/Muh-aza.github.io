About

I work at the intersection of large language models, information extraction, and biomedical informatics. My research focuses on designing robust prompt and retrieval strategies, building knowledge graphs from scientific literature and pathway figures, and developing AI systems that can assist clinicians and researchers with complex decision-making.

Research Interests

A few of the areas I enjoy working in:

  • Large language models, prompt engineering, and prompt optimization.
  • Retrieval-augmented generation and biomedical text / figure mining.
  • Knowledge graphs and representation learning for genes, diseases, and drugs.
